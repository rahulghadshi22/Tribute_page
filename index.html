Captain Vikram Batra

"Yeh Dil Mange More!"

Here is the time line of Captain Vikram Batra's life:

  • 1974 : In September 1974, born in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, India.
  • 1996 : In June 1996, joined the Indian Military Academy (IMA) in Dehradun, Uttarakhand.
  • 1997 : In December 1997, commissioned into the Indian Army as a lieutenant in the 13th Battalion of the Jammu and Kashmir Rifles (13 JAK RIF).
  • 1999 : During the Kargil War, in May 1999, deployed in the Dras sector in Jammu and Kashmir.
  • 1999 : In June 1999,Captain Batra and his team successfully captured Point 5140, a crucial peak in the Kargil region.
  • 1999 : In July 1999, martyred while fighting against Pakistani forces.
  • 1999 : In August 199, posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra, India's highest military decoration.
